CE001

Speaking Production (Conversational)

10 hours $295

Develop speaking skills needed for successful daily communication.

Learning Outcomes

1 Communicate competently with members of the community (peers, colleagues, customers …) at a high level as leads to successful interaction
  • Participate comfortably, actively, and spontaneously in social interactions by expressing opinions, choices, and intention
  • Apply conversational strategies (taking / holding / yielding the floor)
  • Advocate for self. Tactfully apply strategies and language for taking leadership in interactions
2 Produce clear, detailed, accurate speech
  • Apply grammatical structures that add nuance and strengthen the intent of your message
  • Apply language structures accurately when speaking
3 Produce clear, fluent speech by pronouncing and enunciating effectively
  • Approach mastery of sounds
  • Approach mastery of features including vowel reduction, linking, rhythm, stress, and intonation
  • Analyze personal language for sound discrimination and rhythmic features
4 Apply advanced vocabulary effectively in speaking
  • Acquire, retain, and apply advanced, useful vocabulary
  • Apply field-specific and topic-related lexical items, including their collocations
