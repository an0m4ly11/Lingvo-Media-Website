Be understood clearly — every time you speak.

Curriculum

Courses

FE001

Speaking Accurately

10 hours $295

Participants focus on production of vowels and consonants towards becoming more confident speakers.

Learning Outcomes

1 Produce individual sounds clearly and consistently to improve intelligibility
  • Produce consonant and vowel sounds with improved accuracy
  • Differentiate contrastive sounds to reduce ambiguity in academic speech
2 Master phonological processes and enhance sound clarity
  • Apply accurate pronunciation of endings (-s, -ed)
  • Reduce unintelligible substitutions, deletions, or insertions
FE002

Speaking Smoothly

10 hours $295

Focus on rhythm, stress, and intonation patterns to shape meaning and academic and professional credibility.

Learning Outcomes

1 Use correct word and sentence stress to enhance clarity and listener comprehension
  • Apply appropriate stress to highlight key information and contrasts
2 Apply appropriate rhythm, intonation, and thought grouping
  • Produce natural English rhythm through appropriate use of reduction, linking, and timing
  • Use intonation patterns and thought groups to signal structure and transitions in academic speech
