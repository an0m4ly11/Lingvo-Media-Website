Workplace English

Communicate with clarity and confidence at work.

Develop the professional writing and speaking skills required by business and industry — producing clear, context-appropriate communication for real workplace tasks.

Curriculum

Courses

WP001

Professional Writing Skills

10 hours $295

Learn important writing skills towards producing clear, professional, and context-appropriate written communication in real workplace tasks.

Learning Outcomes

1 Develop writing competence to a level required by business and industry
  • Produce workplace documents (e.g. reports, emails, incident summaries) using the conventions and terminology of the target occupation
  • Apply appropriate structure, formatting, and tone for different workplace purposes
2 Develop accuracy, clarity, and audience awareness
  • Use precise vocabulary, technical terms, and concise sentence structures to ensure clarity and reduce ambiguity in professional writing
  • Adapt register, level of detail, and style to suit all stakeholders
WP002

Professional Oral Communication

10 hours $295

Focus on speaking effectively in professional interactions.

Learning Outcomes

1 Develop workplace communicative competence to a level required by business and industry
  • Participate in occupation-specific tasks such as briefings, client interactions, team meetings, or problem-solving discussions
  • Use appropriate questioning, summarizing, and clarifying strategies to ensure accurate information exchange
2 Understand and utilize critical thinking processes and problem-solving techniques
  • Demonstrate professional tone and politeness strategies when interacting with colleagues, supervisors, and clients
  • Use fluency, clear pronunciation, and repair strategies (paraphrasing, rephrasing, confirming) to maintain effective communication
