Curriculum

Courses

IELTS001

Listening

$295

The course develops the listening skills and test-taking strategies needed to understand main ideas, details, and paraphrased information across all IELTS listening sections.

Learning Outcomes

1 Identify key information
  • Students will be able to identify key information, opinions, and supporting details in a range of IELTS-style recordings
2 Apply strategies for accuracy
  • Students will be able to apply prediction, paraphrase recognition, and signposting strategies to improve accuracy on IELTS Listening tasks
IELTS002

Writing

$295

This course prepares learners to produce clear, well-structured responses for both Task 1 and Task 2, with a focus on coherence, accuracy, and academic language.

Learning Outcomes

1 Write task-appropriate responses
  • Students will be able to write organized, task-appropriate responses that meet IELTS expectations for coherence, cohesion, and task achievement
2 Use a wider range of language
  • Students will be able to use a wider range of academic vocabulary and complex grammatical structures with improved accuracy
IELTS003

Speaking

$295

This course builds the fluency, accuracy, and communicative confidence required to perform effectively across all three parts of the IELTS Speaking test.

Learning Outcomes

1 Speak with fluency and coherence
  • Students will be able to speak with improved fluency, coherence, and interactional competence on a range of familiar and abstract topics
2 Express ideas naturally and effectively
  • Students will be able to use clearer pronunciation, a broader lexical range, and more accurate grammar to express ideas naturally and effectively
IELTS004

Reading

$295

Develop the reading strategies and comprehension skills needed to work accurately and efficiently through the full range of IELTS reading question types.

Learning Outcomes

1 Read for meaning and detail
  • Students will be able to identify main ideas, specific details, and writer's opinion across a range of IELTS-style texts
2 Apply efficient reading strategies
  • Students will be able to apply skimming, scanning, and time-management strategies to improve accuracy across IELTS reading question types
