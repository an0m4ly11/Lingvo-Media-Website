Academic English

Speak with clarity in seminars, discussions, and presentations.

Build the spoken academic skills needed to contribute confidently to seminars, lead discussions, and deliver polished, well-structured presentations.

Curriculum

Courses

AE001

Speaking Production (Academic)

10 hours $295

Develop speaking skills needed for successful academic communication including seminars and discussion.

Learning Outcomes

1 Participate effectively in academic seminars and discussions
  • Demonstrate the ability to contribute relevant, well-structured ideas during academic discussions
  • Use turn-taking and clarification strategies to engage in collaborative, critical dialogue
2 Develop accuracy and fluency
  • Produce spoken language with increasing grammatical accuracy and discipline-specific vocabulary
  • Maintain fluency through appropriate self-correction and cohesive devices
AE002

Presentation Delivery (Academic)

10 hours $295

Deliver clear, coherent, and logically organized oral presentations for academic communication such as conference-style talks, research presentations, and seminar briefings.

Learning Outcomes

1 Develop effective verbal delivery skills
  • Use clear articulation, appropriate pacing, and controlled intonation to enhance audience comprehension
  • Employ academic register, discipline-appropriate vocabulary, and cohesive devices to maintain clarity and flow
  • Demonstrate effective non-verbal communication skills
  • Integrate visual aids smoothly and professionally to reinforce key points
2 Develop effective design and interaction skills
  • Construct presentations with a clear introduction, logical progression of ideas, and a concise conclusion
  • Apply academic rhetorical strategies
